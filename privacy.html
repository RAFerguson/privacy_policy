Privacy Policy

For Quick Holdem Poker Pot Odds · Effective date: 1 May 2026

This privacy policy describes how the mobile application Quick Holdem Poker Pot Odds (the “App”), published by Hadrons LLC (“we,” “us”), handles information when you use the App. This is a short policy because the App is designed to do as little as possible with your data.

Information we collect

None. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All calculations happen locally on your device. The App does not require an account, does not connect to any server we control, and does not send analytics or diagnostic data to us.

Information we share

None. Because we do not collect any information, there is nothing to share with third parties, advertisers, analytics providers, or anyone else.

Third-party services

The App does not embed third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or attribution. The App is delivered through the Google Play Store and (if applicable) the Apple App Store; those platforms may collect their own diagnostic information about app installs and crashes under their own privacy policies, which you can review at:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Permissions

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, storage, or any other sensitive device permission.

Children’s privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can confirm no such information exists in our systems.

Educational use only

The App is intended for poker strategy practice and education. It does not facilitate real-money gambling and does not allow you to deposit, withdraw, or wager money of any kind. All inputs and outputs are illustrative.

Data security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no user data on our servers that could be exposed in a breach. Information you enter into the App (pot sizes, bet sizes, draw selections) remains on your device.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after a change indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
